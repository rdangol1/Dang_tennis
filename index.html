About

Tennis is a fun, thrilling and an energetic sport and at Dang's tennis center we take that seriously. Our organization was open in 2010 and have produced numerous outstanding players.

Equipment

We provide a wide range of equipment offering full lines of racquets and accessories. We carry Babolat, Wilson, & Head racquets and accessories. String and re-gripping services are also available.

Staff

Our staff members are always here to help for any inquiries. We have at least 3 staff members on site at any time. We understand the value of your time and are determined to provide outstanding customer service.
